The Arc of Heaven—in progress

The title of this work is excerpted from “The White Birds” by Jümperliin Saruulbuyan. To quote from the eponymous stanza:

“The white birds
dart,
like electricity
they flash by,
perish
in the arc of heaven.”

Saruulbuyan evokes a world in which nature, technology and religion are entangled, interwoven. This is the world I have tried to represent—the world of contemporary Mongolia. I hope to ask, what are the possibilities of modernity, both in Mongolia and at large?
